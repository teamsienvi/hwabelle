What to Look For

When testing, pay attention to:

  1. Accuracy — Does the AI correctly identify uploaded flowers?
  2. Practicality — Are pressing tips actionable and specific to the flower?
  3. Tone — Does it feel warm and friendly, not robotic or overly technical?
  4. Mini press / kids content — Does it mention the mini press when kids or family activities come up?
  5. Drying tools — Does it naturally recommend drying support (silica gel, bamboo charcoal)?
  6. Resin caution — If resin comes up, does it warn about yellowing and suggest test pieces first?
